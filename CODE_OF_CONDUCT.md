# Code of Conduct

T50-Systems projects follow the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in our projects a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and patient.
- Assume good intent, but accept feedback when impact differs from intent.
- Keep discussions focused on the work and the affected users.
- Give and receive technical criticism professionally.
- Respect maintainers' time, project scope, and release priorities.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Trolling, repeated derailment, or bad-faith argument.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. They may also temporarily or permanently limit participation in project spaces.

If you need to report a Code of Conduct concern, contact the maintainers privately through the relevant repository's owner/maintainer channels. Do not disclose sensitive personal information in public issues.

Reports will be reviewed as promptly and fairly as possible. Retaliation against anyone who reports a concern or participates in an investigation is not tolerated.
